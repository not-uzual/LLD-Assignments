Design a Snake and Ladder Game

Requirements:
- User will provide you size of the board, number of snakes, ladders and players and mode of the game.
- Each position contain one of snake or ladder or nothing
- Each player will play in round robin order until someone wins
- Their is two game mode
    - Easy(After 3 six, you will get chance)
    - Haed(After 3 six, chance skipped)
