Design a Snake and Ladder Application

Inputs
- n: board dimension (board size is n x n, cells from 1 to n^2)
- x: number of players
- difficultyLevel: easy or hard

Requirements
- Place exactly n snakes and n ladders randomly on the board.
- Snake: head at higher cell, tail at lower cell.
- Ladder: start at lower cell, end at higher cell.
- Snakes and ladders must not create a cycle.

Game Rules
- Board cells are numbered from 1 to n^2.
- A 6-sided dice (1 to 6) is rolled randomly each turn.
- All players start outside the board at position 0.
- Players play in round-robin order.
- On dice roll d, move from p to p + d.
- If move goes beyond n^2, player does not move.
- If player lands on snake head, move to its tail.
- If player lands on ladder start, move to its end.
- Player wins on reaching exactly n^2.
- Game continues until at least 2 players are still in race to win.
